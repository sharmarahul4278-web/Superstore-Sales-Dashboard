### **2. Short Description / Purpose**

The Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report built to help businesses understand past sales performance and anticipate future demand. The dashboard visualizes key sales metrics, regional performance, and trends while providing a 15-day sales forecast to support better business planning, inventory management, and decision-making.

This dashboard is useful for business analysts, sales managers, retail strategists, and data enthusiasts who want to explore sales patterns and forecast future revenue in an intuitive visual format.

### **5. Features / Highlights**

#### **Business Problem**

Retail businesses often struggle to understand:

* Which states generate the most revenue?
* Which products perform best?
* How sales trends change over time?
* What sales might look like in the next 15 days?

Without an interactive dashboard, these insights are buried in raw data and difficult to interpret quickly.

---

#### **Goal of the Dashboard**

The goal of this dashboard is to:

* Provide a clear visual overview of superstore sales performance.
* Identify top-performing states and products.
* Track sales trends over time.
* Use forecasting to predict sales for the next 15 days.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

---

#### **Walkthrough of Key Visuals**

**Key KPIs (Top Section)**

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Number of Orders
* Average Sales per Order

**State-wise Sales (Bar Chart)**
Shows top-performing states based on total sales, helping identify high-revenue regions.

**Sales Trend Over Time (Line Chart)**
Displays monthly sales trends to understand seasonality and peak periods.

**Category-wise Sales (Donut Chart)**
Breaks down sales by categories such as Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.

**Top 9 States Filter**
A dynamic filter that allows users to focus only on the top 9 revenue-generating states.

**15-Day Sales Forecast (Line Chart with Prediction)**
Uses historical data to predict sales for the next 15 days, helping with inventory planning and demand forecasting.
